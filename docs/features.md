# EHR Application — Feature Roadmap

## 1. Core Clinical

- [ ] **Clinical Notes** — SOAP notes, progress notes, procedure notes, discharge summaries
- [ ] **Order Entry (CPOE)** — labs, imaging, medications, referrals with safety checks (drug interactions, allergy alerts)
- [ ] **Results Management** — lab results, imaging reports, pathology with abnormal flagging and trending
- [ ] **Medication Management** — active medication lists, e-prescribing, refill workflows

## 2. Revenue Cycle & Billing

- [ ] **Coding & Charge Capture** — ICD-10/CPT coding from clinical encounters
- [ ] **Claims Submission** — electronic billing to insurance payers
- [ ] **Eligibility Verification** — real-time insurance coverage checks before visits

## 3. Scheduling & Workflow

- [ ] **Appointment Scheduling** — provider availability, room management, recurring visits
- [ ] **Patient Portal** — self-service booking, results viewing, provider messaging, bill pay
- [ ] **Task Management** — inbox items, referral follow-ups, prior authorizations

## 4. Administrative & Population Health

- [ ] **Registration & Demographics** — patient records, insurance info, emergency contacts
- [ ] **Reporting & Analytics** — quality measures, population health dashboards, regulatory reporting
- [ ] **Clinical Decision Support** — preventive care reminders, drug safety alerts, care gap detection

## 5. Interoperability

- [ ] **Health Information Exchange** — record sharing via HL7/FHIR standards
- [ ] **Referral Management** — specialist referrals with clinical context
- [ ] **Public Health Reporting** — immunization registries, state/federal submissions

## 6. Specialty Modules

- [ ] **Radiology** — imaging orders, PACS integration, structured reporting
- [ ] **Oncology** — treatment protocols, chemotherapy tracking
- [ ] **Surgery** — operative notes, pre-op checklists, anesthesia records
- [ ] **Behavioral Health** — screening tools, treatment plans, session notes
